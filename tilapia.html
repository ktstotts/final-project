Creamy Dill and Lemon Tilapia

Ingredients

  • 4 6oz tilapia fillets, uncooked
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1\2 cup of dill, minced
  • 3 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp of all purpose flour
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Add butter, to a sauce pan on medium heat and melt. Add flour (tbsp at a time), and Stir for 2 minutes. (will look like playdough). Add milk and whisk vigourously until smooth. (pour in 3 increments). Add garlic and dill and season to taste.

  • Place tilapia in a baking pan

  • Take the pot off the heat and use an immersion blender until soup is as smooth as you want. Add nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste.

Ham Bone Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 4 celery stalks, trimmed and sliced
  • 1 large onion, peeled and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 ham bone
  • 2 cups of cubed ham, (cooked)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Â½ head green cabbage, shredded about 8 cups
  • splash of apple cider vinegar
  • 3 cups cooked beans rinsed and drained if canned, such as cannellini, navy or pinto
  • 1 small bunch kale, ribs removed and leaves chopped into bite-size pieces 6 cups
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add the ham, carrots, celery, and onion to the pot. Stir until softened (about 5 mins). Add the garlic and cook for 1 min.

  • Drop in the ham bone and cover with 12 cups of water along with all the other ingredients and simmer for 50 mins. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
